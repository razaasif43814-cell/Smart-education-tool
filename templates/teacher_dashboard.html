Quick guide to using your dashboard
🏆 Leaderboard
Shows students ranked by their best quiz score. Click any student to see their detailed performance.
🔍 Search Students
Use the search bar on the right panel to find a student by name. The leaderboard will also highlight matching students.
📊 Student Detail
Click any student card to view their best score, average score, and total quizzes taken.
🟢 Status Dot
Green dot = student has taken at least one quiz. Grey dot = no quizzes taken yet.
